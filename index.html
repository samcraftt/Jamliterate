Upcoming Features

Userbase System: On Jamliterate, you will be able to create an account and save your data for a more personalized learning experience each time you log in.

Flash Draw: Our mobile application will allow you to take stylus-written notes on the fly, so you don't have to turn your class notes into flashcards later.

Intelligent Study Buddy: This AI-powered study tool will help you prepare for common standardized tests, providing specific types of questions based on your strengths and weaknesses.

Card Composer: To waste no time creating review materials, users will be able to feed our AI Card Composer text for it to automatically build a comprehensive flashcard set.

Rapid Review: For our procrastinators and crammers, we plan to implement a tool that condenses a large flashcard set into a smaller set that groups together the main ideas for last-minute studying.
